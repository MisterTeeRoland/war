WAR!!!

The game is simple. Compare your card against the computer. Highest card wins, and winner takes both cards. Play until one person has all of the cards. Press PLAY to begin!

In the event of a tie, THERE WILL BE WAR. Three cards will be drawn from each deck (if applicable) and the fourth card from each will be compared. Winner takes all cards.
